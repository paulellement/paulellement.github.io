About Me

Paul Ellement is an esteemed actor, director, artist, painter, astronaut, and two-time iron-man triathlon world champion. Actually, no he isn't. He's a computer science and music technology student at that university in Montreal, Canada. He regularly attends McGill Outdoors Club meetings and was a player on their execs' inner-tube waterpolo team before he decided to trip and faceplant into his hockey bench and get concussed. Paul spends his summers guiding whitewater canoe trips at Camp Kandalore. He will be guiding some kids down La Rivière du Chef this summer, a 26-day trip in Québec. He also recently learned to roll a kayak! He worked at Kandalore's Outdoor Education Centre in the springs of 2022 and 2023, where he taught canoeing and mountaing biking, ran the high-ropes course, and belayed rock climbers.
